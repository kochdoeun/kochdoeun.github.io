I am KOCH Doeun. I was born in 1990. I am from Takeo province. I have two sisters and one brother. My two sisters are factory workers, my younger brother is a student and my parents are farmers.

The life of high school was not easy for me because my family is poor, so all spending needs me to find by myself to support my study. When I had free time from school, I took time to work as constructive worker and also in the evening, I taught English for students in private school, so I could have some money to spend for my study and left some money for my brother’s study. Furthermore, I had successfully passed the 2-year scholarship to study in Information Technology (IT) field as what I had dreamed at Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC) which was known as Centre for Information System Training (CIST); This organization is to help underprivileged young Cambodia people to study and have a better job in ICT. Learning at PNC, I had got a warm and hopeful life. It is because I met many good friends from different provinces around Cambodia and what the most important is we are from the same background.

I have been trained a lot of subjects not only Web Programming, but also Business Life (professional life, company knowledge and general knowledge), then I have undergone internship twice; two months for Cambodia Business Coalition on Aids Organization as Web Developer and four months for Mapring Co.; Ltd as Web Developer too.

For bachelor degree, I have gotten one-year scholarship from D.K.KIM Foundation to apply for University of Puthisastra. In this university, I studied in the evening shift after work. I went to work and learnt by cycle so I needed to spend long hours getting university.

For master degree, I am curently learning the master of scient information technology at Norton University.
